Please Note: Kosher astrology does not predict the future, and will not tell you whether you should or shouldn’t do something. For example, if you ask, “Should I quit my job?” Do not expect a “yes or no” answer. I may point out that you have a major new astrological beginning coming up in six months, and it kicks off a fresh start for the next 30 years of your life! You can take that information into consideration in terms of whether to quit your job. Especially if you want to start something new that is more in alignment with your natural gifts.

Do not expect any “yes or no” answers from astrology. Rather, you may receive lots of information that can inform your decision-making process. There is a lot of info about our life’s direction and purpose contained in our birth chart. I am happy to explore it with you and work together to see what it might be saying about your life and purpose.
