The Seven Pivotal Periods

Astrologically, we all have seven specific times in our lives when our core-self, our essence, and our identity may go through significant transformations. The timing of these periods varies for each person.

For some, they are spread out throughout their lifetime; for others, they are more closely bunched together. Some people experience two or three of these astrological pivotal periods occurring at the same time in their lives.

For example, if a person has a few pivotal periods when they are twenty-seven years old, this suggests that at age twenty-seven this person will be going through a particularly powerful and notable life-changing growth opportunity.

When big changes occur in our lives, it is very common to ask: “Why is this happening to me?”

By studying the specific astrological energy associated with each pivotal period, we can discover the underlying purpose for the transformative events taking place. This may help us understand how these new developments are here to assist our personal evolution and growth.

The ROADMAP will not tell you specific details about what will transpire at those times. Rather, they will highlight different parts of yourself that are potentially ripe for change and growth during a specific time period in your life.
It can help you understand what is happening to you, and how it can help you grow.

The Lifetime Cycles ( = 12 Turning Points)
The Twelve Lifetime Turning Points
Unlike the seven Pivotal Periods, the twelve Lifetime Turning Points can only happen one turning point at a time. They also only occur at evenly spaced intervals throughout our lives. Each turning point has a different energy and represents a different area of our personal development, and they usually signify that we are ready for a major shift in our lives.
It is usually at a turning point when something of great significance to us shifts or changes.
We can literally map out the major developments of our lives with the exact astrological date of each of these twelve turning points
