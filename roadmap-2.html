The Transits

A Transiting Planet connecting with your birth chart indicates a momentous growth opportunity, that can deeply affect you. Some of life’s greatest challenges, events, and transformations may take place during these major growth periods of your life

1. Your core-self, your identity (Sun),
2. Your emotions, your wants and desires (Moon),
3. Your life’s purpose (Nodes)
