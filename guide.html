Part 3: Astrology In The Torah

Did you know that some of our most illustrious leaders and Rabbis were expert astrologers?!

  • Avraham Avinu, Shmuel (the Tanna), Rabbi Shabbetai Donnolo, Rabbi Avraham bar Chiyya, the Ibn Ezra, the Ralbag (Gersonides), and many others – were all expert astrologers!
  • Others, such as Rabbi Yehudah HaChassid, Rabbeinu Bachya, the Ran, Rabbi Yitzchak Arama (Akeidas Yitzchak), Abarbanel, Rama, Ramchal, Chida, the B’nei Yissascher, and many others, all firmly believed in astrology and wrote extensively about it in their seforim (books)!
In Part 3, the book delves into the astrological worldview of various great Rabbis and the shocking connections they make between astrology and the Torah.
